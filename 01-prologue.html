🪶 The First Vault

"Before there were dice, there was the hum.
Before Beetles gleamed in mortal hands, the vaults dreamed of being found."
— The Vaultkeeper’s Chronicle, Entry I

Welcome, Vaultwalker.

You step into a tradition older than any empire — a contest of fortune, courage, and cunning.
The vaults you see scattered across the board are not mere buildings; they are fragments of an ancient system once designed to measure worth.
Each vault remembers every player who ever claimed it. Each Beetle that moves between your fingers carries echoes of those who lost theirs.

🪲 The World Beneath the Dice​

When you roll, the world responds.
Vaults awaken, Gargoyles stir, and the Beetles — sacred tokens of balance — begin their quiet exchange between fate and fortune.
Every vault claimed shapes the board, and the board shapes you in return.
You are not simply playing — you are remembering the old war between chance and control.

🧭 The Path Ahead​

As you continue through this codex, you will learn:

  • The Path of the Vaultwalker, where choices define destiny.
  • The Natures of the Vaults, each governed by an element and temperament.
  • The Lore of Beetles, currency and lifeblood of the vaults.
  • The Covenant of Gargoyles, stone guardians bound to test your resolve.
  • And the Rival, who mirrors your ambition and thirst for power.

Each lesson comes at a cost — the Beetles themselves are the price of remembrance.

So listen well, apprentice.
Claim wisely, roll bravely, and never forget:

“The vaults remember everything… even you.”
