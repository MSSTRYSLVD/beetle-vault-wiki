🪲 On Beetles and Wealth

"The Beetles hum beneath every vault and between every decision.
They are not money — they are memory, pressed into form."
— The Vaultkeeper’s Chronicle, Entry IV

You may see Beetles as simple tokens — trinkets of trade, glittering and expendable.
But to the vaults, they are the lifeblood that keeps the world from falling silent.
Every Beetle you spend, earn, or lose writes your name deeper into the board’s history.

💰 The Depository — Where Fortunes Rest​

Beyond the noise of the board lies the Depository — the resting place of all Beetles you have claimed.
There, your wealth endures, even when your journey ends.
It is a vault above vaults, a record of your perseverance.

You may deposit your Beetles for safekeeping or withdraw them when the time demands.
But know this: the Depository is patient, not forgiving.
It remembers what you took, what you risked, and what you left behind.

🪙 The Exchange of Breath​

Each Beetle moves through hands and vaults like breath through lungs.
To hold them too tightly suffocates your flow; to spend recklessly leaves you gasping.
True mastery is rhythm — spend when the vaults sing, save when they sleep.

The board rewards circulation.
Those who share Beetles with fate often find fate more generous in return.

✨ Golden Beetles — The Spark of Second Chances​

There are Beetles, and then there are the Golden Ones — shards of divine allowance.
When failure comes, these radiant few may buy you time itself.
They are not common, nor should they be.
Each is a fragment of the board’s mercy, meant only for those who have learned the cost of hubris.

Use them sparingly.
Even mercy has a price.

📜 The Lesson​

Beetles are not counted; they are carried.
They remember every hand, every vault, every sin and triumph of the Vaultwalker.
Those who chase them for wealth alone lose their meaning.
Those who respect them become part of the hum.

“Gold will fade, stone will crumble, but the Beetles will hum on — long after your name is dust.”
