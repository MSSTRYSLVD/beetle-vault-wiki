🏰 Vaults and Their Natures

"A vault is not a structure. It is a temperament wearing stone and color."
— The Vaultkeeper’s Chronicle, Entry III

Every vault upon the board holds a personality of its own.
Some whisper promises, others roar warnings.
Each demands payment, devotion, or sacrifice.

To master the vaults is to understand their nature — the pulse of their element.

🏰 Castle Vaults — The Pride of Stone​

The Castle Vaults are fortresses of fortune, where wealth and arrogance sleep side by side.
Their walls gleam with the Beetles of those who overpaid and overreached.

  • Nature: Stable, heavy, proud.
  • Reward: High rent and safety from chaos.
  • Risk: Costly to claim — they test your greed before your strength.

Castle Vaults respect patience.
Those who rush to seize them often find themselves poor before the second roll.

🌊 Water Vaults — The Flow of Fortune​

Beneath the glass surface of the Water Vaults lies a current that never rests.
Fortunes shift here like tides — one turn they swell, the next they recede.

  • Nature: Fluid, unpredictable.
  • Reward: Variable income — luck can turn profit into peril.
  • Risk: Ever-changing; they mirror your rival’s aggression and your hesitation alike.

Water Vaults are best for those who can surf the unknown.
Do not anchor too deeply — the tide remembers what it takes.

🔥 Fire Vaults — The Trial of Will​

Fire Vaults burn for dominance.
To stand within one is to test your nerve against the board itself.
They devour Beetles from the arrogant and gift them to the daring.

  • Nature: Destructive, intoxicating.
  • Reward: Can ruin or empower in a single roll.
  • Risk: One misstep, and your fortune turns to ash.

Fire does not favor the brave. It favors the aware.
Learn when to burn and when to step aside.

🗿 Gargoyle Vaults — The Sentinels of Stone​

Gargoyle Vaults are not owned — they are respected.
Ancient guardians slumber within, awakened by those foolish enough to challenge them.
Defeat one, and it will yield treasures beyond Beetles: fragments of power carved from its own form.

  • Nature: Ancient, watchful.
  • Reward: Gargoyle Fragments — relics that fortify your holdings.
  • Risk: Failure costs Beetles and pride alike.

Every Gargoyle you face remembers you.
Every fragment you win whispers of the next challenge yet to come.

🧭 The Lesson​

Each vault is a mirror of your intent:

  • The Castle reflects your ambition.
  • The Water reflects your adaptability.
  • The Fire reflects your courage.
  • The Gargoyle reflects your soul.

“Vaults are not conquered. They are conversed with. Listen well, and they may answer in Beetles.”
